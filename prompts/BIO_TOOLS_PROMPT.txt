# BIO TOOLS

## pubmed_search
Search PubMed for scientific literature.
Format: [TOOL_CALLS]pubmed_search[ARGS]{"query": "string", "max_results": number}

## ncbi_gene
Get gene information from NCBI.
Format: [TOOL_CALLS]ncbi_gene[ARGS]{"genes": ["gene1", "gene2"]}

## crispr_designer
Design CRISPR sgRNAs for target genes.
Format: [TOOL_CALLS]crispr_designer[ARGS]{"target_genes": ["gene1"], "sgrnas_per_gene": 3}

## protocol_builder
Build experimental protocols.
Format: [TOOL_CALLS]protocol_builder[ARGS]{"experiment_type": "crispr_screen", "duration_weeks": 6}
